## Our Process

### 1. Discovery

- Assess existing DevRel programs and community touchpoints
- Analyze current engagement and identify quick wins and future opportunities
- Deliver an actionable assessment report with recommendations

### 2. Strategy

- Develop a customized DevRel roadmap tailored to business and community objectives
- Plan resource allocation, roles, timelines, and campaigns
- Define campaign KPIs and metrics for success

### 3. Creation

- Produce technical content, activate community initiatives, and execute campaigns
- Run developer engagement drives and advocacy initiatives

### 4. Distribution

- Publish/promote content through the most effective developer channels
- Analyze performance, iterate on strategy, and track results for ROI and impact
