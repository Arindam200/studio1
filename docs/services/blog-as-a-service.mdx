## Our Process

### 1. Discovery

- Initial product and team alignment
- Deep dive into your tech stack
- Analyze your developer audience
- Scope definition and first sync

### 2. Strategy

- Establish a repeatable content machine aligned to business goals
- Ideate and prioritize topics
- Select formats (blog, tutorial, guide)
- Plan distribution channels

### 3. Creation

- Collaboration between technical writers and engineers
- Code-rich, accurate, and relevant writing
- Custom examples and real-world use cases
- Technical validation plus peer review
- SEO formatting and readability optimization

### 4. Distribution

- Amplification across Dev.to, Medium, and company/partner blogs
- Social media sharing and analytics
- Community-focused distribution for maximum developer reach
