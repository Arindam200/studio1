## What is Memori?

<Frame>![Memori product overview](/images/memori.png)</Frame>

[**Memori**](https://github.com/MemoriLabs/Memori) is an open-source AI memory layer for **LLM applications, AI agents, and multi-agent systems**. It helps developers add persistent, SQL-native memory to their stack without forcing a new architecture or workflow.

Because Memori sits close to core product behavior, adoption depends heavily on **developer trust**. Builders need to understand not just the feature set, but the real use-cases, workflows, and why memory matters in production AI systems.

## The challenge

Memori needed a growth motion that could:

- Introduce the product to the right developer audiences early
- Create trust through **high-signal, technical storytelling**, not generic promotion
- Turn community discussions into **product and documentation feedback**
- Help developers understand the product faster through **hands-on demos**
- Scale awareness without depending on paid campaigns

The goal was not just attention. It was to build an early community around Memori and convert that attention into **stars, feedback, onboarding improvements, and roadmap clarity**.
